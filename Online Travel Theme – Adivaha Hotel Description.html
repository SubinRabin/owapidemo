    Property Description

    Property Location
    With a stay at Hotel Bright, you'll be centrally located in New Delhi, just a 4-minute walk from Kasturba Gandhi Marg and 15 minutes by foot from Jantar Mantar. This hotel is 1.9 mi (3 km) from India Gate and 1.9 mi (3.1 km) from National Museum.

    Rooms
    Make yourself at home in one of the 10 air-conditioned rooms featuring minibars and LCD televisions. Complimentary wireless Internet access keeps you connected, and satellite programming is available for your entertainment. Private bathrooms with showers feature complimentary toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include phones, as well as safes and desks.

    Amenities
    Take in the views from a rooftop terrace and a garden and make use of amenities such as complimentary wireless Internet access. Additional features at this hotel include concierge services and a picnic area.

    Dining
    Take advantage of the hotel's 24-hour room service. A complimentary continental breakfast is included.

    Business, Other Amenities
    Featured amenities include a 24-hour business center, dry cleaning/laundry services, and a 24-hour front desk. A roundtrip airport shuttle is provided for a surcharge (available on request), and free self parking is available onsite.

    I very nearly cancelled my reservation for this hotel - having read a lot of shady reviews and having only paid £28 a night I was suspicious. I am so glad I didn't. The pre-arranged driver met us at the airport. He was so good, that he drove us around Delhi each day we were there, very knowledgeable, safe and friendly. Upon arrival in Connaught Place, which is super crowded and packed with traffic, go past the friendly security guard and up the stairs. This takes you to the 'disputed landing' but don't worry about this. Once you open the door on the right to the hotel, you'll be glad you came. The entire building is finished to a very modern standard, all the amenities you'd expect, including good Wifi, a safe and air conditioning. Complementary welcome drinks, were given on arrival and breakfast is included. I'm not a fan of Indian spiced eggs, or many of the options they offered, but that's personal taste. The room and bathroom were always spotlessly clean, fresh bedding and towels. Shower had good pressure and reliable hot water. You will read that there is a nightclub nearby. This is true, however the music always stopped early, around 10:30pm, so this really is not an issue. The staff went above and beyond to make us feel welcome, calling us each time our driver, even at 02:30am for the Taj tour, was ready to pick us up Remember, all this for £29 a night! Delhi is by no means a walkable city, however, around Connaught Place there are many shops and restaurants. If you're coming to Delhi, look no further than this hotel.
